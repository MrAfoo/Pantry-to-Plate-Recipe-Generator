## 💼 LinkedIn Post

**Title / Hook:**
> I built an AI chef that looks inside your fridge and cooks up 3 restaurant-quality recipes in seconds 🍳

**Full Post:**
> Ever stared into your fridge with no idea what to cook?
>
> I built **Pantry to Plate** — an AI-powered recipe generator that actually *sees* your ingredients.
>
> Here's how it works:
> 📸 Drop 1–5 photos of your fridge, pantry, or ingredients
> 🔍 The AI scans every item — labels, packaging, fresh produce, leftovers
> 🍽️ Get 3 personalised recipes instantly:
>    • 🥄 A simple everyday dish
>    • ⭐ An impressive, flavourful meal
>    • 🍚 A rice or flatbread dish using what you have
>
> It also handles dietary preferences (keto, vegan, gluten-free, halal, and more) — and if key ingredients are missing, it gives you a minimal grocery list with a friendly message like *"Just pick up chicken breast and you can enjoy a full keto stir-fry tonight!"*
>
> Built with:
> → Next.js 15 (App Router)
> → Groq API + Llama 4 Scout Vision (multimodal AI)
> → Tailwind CSS
> → Upstash Redis (for community recipe ratings)
> → jsPDF (download your recipe as a PDF)
> → PWA — install it on your phone like a native app
>
> It's live, free, and open source. Try it and let me know what's in your fridge 👇
